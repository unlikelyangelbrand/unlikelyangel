FAQs

+ THE MEANING BEHIND THE NAME

"Unlikely Angel" is a reminder to look closer. We believe that the people society often overlooks are the ones with the most to offer. Our brand is a tribute to the overlooked, the underestimated, and the divine found in the everyday.

+ SHIPPING & ORDER TRACKING

All physical products are handled through our Etsy storefront to ensure secure transactions and reliable tracking. Typical processing time is 3-5 business days. You will receive an automated tracking number the moment your order ships.

+ HOW WE GIVE BACK (THE MOVEMENT)

A portion of every purchase goes directly toward our Outreach Days. We hand-deliver care packages, clothing, and resources to the unhoused community in Venice Beach and beyond. We aren't just a brand; we are a mission in motion.

+ RESTOCKS & LIMITED EDITIONS

To maintain the quality and exclusivity of our pieces, most drops are limited edition. Once a specific design sells out, it rarely returns in the same format. Follow our updates for early access to upcoming releases.
